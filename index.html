[ Charity ] Karing Frontiers

#7

Developed full payment flow for free

Karing Frontiers is a charity that aim to raise 10,966 USD per year for the Mae Ma Moe Junior College. I reached out to the founders of the charity regarding whether they would be interested in a completely free site to really empower their fundraising capabilities.

Prior to my involvement, they were completely dependent upon a personal PayPal donation flow that was unreliable and unscalable. I completely re-developed the site from scratch and implemented a Stripe powered payment flow using Python, specifically Flask.

The payment flow is 100% secure, responsive and user friendly. It also supports Apple Pay and provides us with a great deal of information about our donors, all while being compliant with EU data laws and payment processing standards.

The source code for this project is not available due to its sensitive nature.

BEM Convention

All of my projects from this point onwards will be following the BEM convention.

BEM is a convention that states how classes should be named to avoid overlap and keep code clean and digestible.

Put simply, it keeps everything organised for the current developer or future developers to pick back up and work on in the future. As well as preventing a lot of errors and making troubleshooting significantly easier.
